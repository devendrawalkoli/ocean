Mantis Shrimp

These bizarre and colorful-looking shrimp have 12 color receptors in their eyes, as compared to the three we humans possess. It’s a gorgeous and creepy-looking animal.

2

Marrus Orthocanna

This colonial animal is composed of a complex arrangement of zooids, some of which are polyps and some medusae. At its front is an orange-colored, gas-filled float that looks like fire.

3

Deep Sea Jellyfish

Deep sea jellyfish come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, and are found in every ocean in the world. Still, this deep sea version is pretty hard to come by because they live way down deep.

4

Narwhal

The narwhal is a really interesting creature. And no, they’re not swimming unicorns, but they certainly look like they could be! Those long, nerve-filled tusks help the narwhal better sense its environment.
