Dolphin

Dolphins are small-toothed cetaceans. There are 36 dolphin species, found in every ocean. Most dolphins are marine and live in the ocean or brackish waters along coastlines.

Blue Whale

The blue whale is the largest animal on the planet, weighing as much as 200 tons. Its stomach can hold one ton of krill and it needs to eat about four tons of krill each day.
